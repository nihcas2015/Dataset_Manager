Data Conqueror

Data Conqueror is an AI-driven tool designed to automate dataset retrieval and preparation. With an intuitive conversational UI, a powerful orchestrator agent, and smart acquisition capabilities, it helps researchers and engineers acquire and preprocess data with ease.

  • Data Acquisition: Automates dataset discovery, retrieval, and compliance-based collection.
  • Data Preprocessing: Cleans, transforms, and prepares raw data for downstream AI/ML tasks.
